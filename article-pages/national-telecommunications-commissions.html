National Telecommunications Commissions

EIGEL Management Consulting worked with the National Telecommunications Commission - Quezon City, NCR that works with the Consultancy Service to facilitate and conduct Strategic Planning and Programming of the 2018 Budget. National Telecommunications Commission, Central Office attended by regional directors.

Company/Government Unit Background

The National Telecommunications Commission’s main objective is to regulate installment, operation and management of public and private radio stations around the country. Another use includes the regulation and supervision of the provision of public telecommunications services. It is tasked to manage the radio spectrum and to manage both radio and television broadcast stations, cable television (CATV) and lastly, pay television.

Project Objectives

The project aims to provide a consultancy service, to facilitate and conduct Strategic Planning and Programming of the sector’s budget for the year 2018.
