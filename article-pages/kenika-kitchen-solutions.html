Philippine Chamber of Commerce & Industry - Quezon City

EIGEL Management Consulting worked with Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation with a project that aims to train the company for Quality Management Systems through several seminars and workshops that promotes awareness of ISO 9001:2015 as well as create an opportunity for ISO Certification. The success of the project is made possible by the cooperation and determination of both companies in participation and involvement.

Company/Government Unit Background

Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corp., in partnership with ACE Filters International, is the leading filter paper provider of fast-food chains in the Philippines. This ISO 9001 certified company also offers complete packages of products and services of kitchen solutions, from equipment sales to service repairs. ACE Filters International, and Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation in turn, are committed in finding ways to reduce the negative environmental impact of their productions, and services. Currenty, Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation’s facilities and/or offices can be found in Quezon City, and in Pampanga.

Project Objectives

The project with Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corp. aims to provide knowledge in different aspects of management by conducting the following activities:

  • First Management Review
    • To discuss the management of different divisions of Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation. This includes evaluation of the effectiveness and adequacy of the Quality Management System of the organization and formulate improvements as an opportunity for the organization.
  • ISO 9001:2015 Awareness Training and Workshop
    • To provide adequate training and workshop for Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation for ISO 9001:2015. The meeting prepares Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation for ISO Certification starting with the familiarization of the concept of ISO 9001:2015 until the discussion on the necessary requirements to comply with ISO 9001:2015.

Roadmap of the Project

With the help of EIGEL Management Consulting, Kenika Kitchen Solutions Corporation has successfully completed its First Management Review. The goal of the Management Review was to assess the existing policies, systems, and objectives of the organization. During the management review, EIGEL Management Consulting aided in the improvement of the management system as a whole. Along with the ISO 9001:2015 Awareness Training and Workshop, Kenika became a more refined and successful ISO certified organization.
