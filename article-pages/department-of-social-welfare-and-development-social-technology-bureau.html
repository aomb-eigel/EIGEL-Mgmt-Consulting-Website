Department of Social Welfare and Development Social Technology Bureau

EIGEL Management Consulting worked with the Department of Social Welfare and Development - Social Technology Bureau Quezon City, NCR with the project that works with the development and preparation of manual preparation, conduct of team building; performance evaluation workshop on what went well and what went wrong, and facilitate and conduct various trainings and workshops for the Development, Integration and Implementation of Quality Management System (QMS) under ISO 9001:2015. The Social Technology Bureau is the Think Tank of all the bureau of the DSWD.

Company/Government Unit Background

The Department of Social Welfare and Development - Social Technology Bureau is mandated by the law to develop, implement and coordinate social protection and poverty reduction solutions for and with the poor, children, youth, older persons, persons with disabilities, and communities that needs assistance. It is the city government's welfare department, and it is dedicated to developing and implementing comprehensive programs and services aimed at empowering people, groups, and communities to become self-sufficient.

Project Objectives

The project aims to conduct various seminars, workshops, and training for the Development, Integration and Implementation of Quality Management System (QMS) under ISO 9001:2015.

  • Coordination Meeting
    • To assist in the design and development of the Quality Management System (QMS) of Social Technology Bureau (STB) per Executive Order 605 (2006); to facilitate the appointment of focal person and ISO team. The scope of the meeting is the design and development of the QMS of STB aligned under ISO 9001:2015.
