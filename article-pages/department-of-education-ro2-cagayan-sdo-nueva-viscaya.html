Department of Education RO2 Cagayan SDO – Nueva Viscaya

The establishment of DepED Regional Office 02, Cagayan Valley Region traces its early beginnings when it conformed to the Integrated Reorganization Plan of the government mandated by Proclamation No. 1081 in September 24, 1972. In 1975, a massive reorganization was implemented. Thirteen regional offices were created including Cagayan Valley Region or Region 02.
