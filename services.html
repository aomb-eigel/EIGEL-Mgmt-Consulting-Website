QUALITY MANAGEMENT & SYSTEMS

We will assist you in achieving quality, safe, and efficient systems by guiding the internal and external processes of your organization through ISO standards.

  • Quality Management Systems (ISO 9001-2015)
  • Environmental Management Systems (ISO 14001:2015)
  • Occupational Health and Safety Management (ISO 45001:2018)
  • Risk Identification, Documentation and Control (ISO 31000:2018)
  • Internal Audit (ISO 1901:2018)
  • Patent and Industrial Design Registration
  • Foods and Drugs Registration
  • Corporate Strategic Panning cum Team Building
  • Power Generation and Management

DEVELOPMENT PLANNING

We can help you formulatine and enhance your comprehensive development plans as well as create feasibility reports to reach your target objectives.

  • Comprehensive Land Use Plan (CLUP)
  • Comprehensive Development Plan (CDP)
  • Local Climate Change Adoption (LCCAP)
  • Biodiversity Management Plan
  • Irrigation Water Resource Management Plan
  • Identification of Financial Outsourcing
  • Feasibility Study Reports
  • Forest Land Use Plan
  • Protected Area of Management
  • People's Survival Fund
  • Other Thematic Plans

ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE & MANAGEMENT

We will get you your environmental permits and licenses as well as minimize the environmental and social impacts of your development projects.

  • Preparation of Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Initial Environmental Examination (IEEE) for securing Environmental Compliance (ECC)
  • Secure Environmental (Permits to Operate, Hazardous Waste Generator ID, Discharge Permit), and other allied environmental permits and licenses
  • Preparation of Self-Monitoring (SMR) and Compliance Monitoring (CMR)
