Geraldine Y. Estioko

Senior Consultant

Geraldine Y. Estioko graduated from the University of Santo Tomas with a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering. She is currently one of the senior consultants at the EIGEL Management Consulting. She has proven competencies in technical management, quality system, environmental system, and customer relation with her completed projects.

She has been with the GOTOH Philippines Corporation for 26 years where she has developed her knowledge of manufacturing in Plating, Rinsing, Engineering, and Laboratory. During her stay with the semiconductor company, she has been assigned with managing Quality Assessment activities aligned with ISO 9001 and IATF 16949. She is responsible for the chemical process planning and cost analysis of the corporation. Appointed as a managing head as she directs the environmental management activities with Water Treatment Facilities and oversees the maintenance of the plant facilities operation. In her projects with the EIGEL Management Consulting, she has been assigned with environmental projects of ECC amendment of Mindoro Medical Center, and ECC Formulation and Application for SNAP-Magat Inc. and SOMOS Bansud Hospital.

She attended seminars of ISO 19011:2011 Guidelines for Auditing Management System for ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 Awareness Training. She has skills with project management as she also attended seminars of Managerial Effectiveness and Eight Hour Training for Managing Heads. In her years with the semiconductor industry and EIGEL Management Consulting, she has developed skills with Quality Management Audit, Environmental System Audit, Project Management, and Products, Process and Machine Qualification.
