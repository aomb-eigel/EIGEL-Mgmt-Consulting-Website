Catherine C. Cena

Senior Consultant

Catherine C. Cena is one of the Associate Consultants and the Team Leader in Environmental Management Systems (EMS) of EIGEL Management Consulting. She obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Ecological and Environmental Engineering at Western Mindanao State University and her Master’s degree in Environmental Science at De la Salle University Dasmariñas.

Specializing in environmental management and compliance, Catherine Cena finished the following projects in EIGEL Management Consulting: 1.) ECC application of Sto. Tomas Batangas where she also helped in writing the EIA 2.) ECC application of LAMCI Bulacan, Prime Medical Doctors, and Great Valley Medical Center, 3.) CMR online reporting for LAMCI, and 4.) Happy Well site visit for the environmental impact of deep well water.

Her skills and experiences regarding Environmental management and compliance are rooted in her employment as Department Technical Support Staff with Cleanway Technology Corporation then promoted as Operations Officer and Pollution Control Officer and an appointed Documentation Control Officer. Currently, she Supervises and handles the Admin Services group.

Other than being one of the Associate Consultants at EIGEL Management Consulting, she is also currently a Pollution Control Officer, Safety Officer, GAD Head, ISO Committee head for IMS, and Supervisor at USHIO PHILS., INC. Also, she became part of companies such as Megawide Construction Corporation as a Pollution Control Officer/ Safety Officer, Cleanway Technology Corporation as Pollution Control Officer/ ISO-DCC, and Permitting and Compliance Leader.

She managed to obtain certifications as an IRCA Certified Auditor, Accredited Pollution Control Officer, and Trained Safety Officer. With these other qualifications, her skills, and professional experiences, Catherine Cena became one of the deemed Consultants in EIGEL Management Consulting.
