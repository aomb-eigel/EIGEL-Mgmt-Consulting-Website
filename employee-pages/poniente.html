Alyssa Mae U. Poniente

Senior Consultant

Alyssa Mae Poniente is one of the leading consultants in EIGEL Management Consulting. She is a registered Civil Engineer and Environmental Planner. She obtained her Bachelor’s degree at Lyceum of the Philippines University- Cavite and is currently achieving her Master's Degree in Urban and Regional Planning at the University of the Philippines - Diliman. Alyssa specializes in engineering and local planning.

Nurturing her love for planning, she became part of the City Planning and Development Coordinator’s office at the City Government of General Trias wherein she became a Project Development Officer. With her position, she experienced conceptualizing and preparing detailed architectural and engineering designs and computations. Furthermore, she experienced being a Project focal person in the formulation of the first local Public Transportation Route Plan of the City of General Trias. Other than those, she also experienced evaluating Traffic Impact Assessments, being the focal-person for updating the City Comprehensive Land Use Plan and City Ecological Profile, Project focal-person for the conducting of the City Climate and Disaster Risk Assessment, and Project focal-person for the updating of the City Comprehensive Development Plan.

Other than her job experience, her career also includes attending training and seminars. These training and seminars are Traffic Impact Assessment for Technical Evaluators, Modernization of the Philippine Geodetic Reference System, Local Public Transportation Route Planning and Management Course, Training on Data Gathering for the Local Migration Information System, and Climate and Disaster Risk Assessment Seminar and Workshop. All these experiences and training aided her in becoming one of the best planners and consultants in EIGEL Management Consulting.
